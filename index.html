About

Net Café Account System monitoring using RFID and GSM Module for SMS Notification


The development of technology nowadays is already part of human life. It also provides additional factor to those enterprises who develop new businesses. It is suitable for customers, especially in business to customer commerce. The users can use their RFID card to log-in via internet and make payment in short time period. RFID system technology is believed to be a revolutionary addition to mobile payment systems.

Who We Are?

What We Do?

We are developing a RFID card to log-in via internet and make payment in short time period.

Why We Do It?

To improve the payment method of an Internet shop. Through the help of RFID, it lessen the hassle of paying cash.

Since When?

It started since the beginning of our Capstone summer class and still improving till this day.


Project Context


The Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) refers to an Auto-Identification machine created from RFID tags, readers and the needful middleware that translates tag records and communicates it to the software. RFID tags comprise particular item records of their memory, accessed through the radio sign of an RFID reader. A GSM modem or GSM module is a tool that makes use of GSM cell cellphone generation to offer a Wi-Fi facts hyperlink to a network. GSM modems are utilized in cell phones and different devices that communicate with cell cellphone networks. They use SIMs to perceive their tool to the network.

Services

Services we Offer

Top-up

Through the help of RFID, it lessen the hassle of paying cash. Aside from that, by using RFID card users will be able to view and monitor their account.

Promo's

Customers may quickly discover what promotions are available on the system's website. In addition, using the application and scanning or typing user’s RFID code, such as load balance, the client can check the balance by using RFID card in that application.

Easy Access

Once the user’s information matched with information stored in central database system, then user can enter the username and password. If a match is found, access is granted to the user.

No Queuing

There is no need to wait for an available computer to be used. In fact no need for the owner of the internet cafe's assistance because, it is similar to an internet cafe where only the computer slot is required, and you can log in and use the computer with your RFID card.
